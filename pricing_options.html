2. What is the Bitcoin Development Kit?

Simply put, the idea of a Bitcoin Development Kit is a collection of functions that are written in PHP programming language (soon to support many other languages, C, Java, Ruby, Python, etc)

But,This library isn't just a few simple "wrappers" for a connection to the Bitcoin Client ( Also known as, Bitcoind ). Our team has put an immensive amount of research effort towards security, usefulness, functionality and most importantly stability.

The BDK was built to be flexable from the ground up, so it really applys to anyone that would like to(or needs to) integrate the Bitcoin network into their next project. Some specific use cases would include:

* Accept Bitcoins through your ecommerce shop

* Allow anyone on your message board to be tipped(or to tip others)

* Provide and escrow service

* Usefull for your next video game currency

and that does not even touch the surface when it comes to the amount of vast power this beast contains!
